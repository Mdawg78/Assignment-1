Who celebrates it

Songkran is mostly celebrated in Thailand but its also prominent in countries like Cambodia, Myanmar and Laos.

When it takes place

It takes place starting from 13 April until 15 April, where "Thais across the country cool off and celebrate new beginnings with what is arguably the nation's largest and most important festival".(Lynn, 2025).

WHat it signifies

It is a way for them to wash off their misfortunes and become pure. It is also a way for them to honour their ancestors and elders.

How it is celebrated

It is celebrated with a lot people splashing one another with water, most of the times with water guns.
